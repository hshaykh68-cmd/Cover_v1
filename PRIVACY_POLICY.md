# Cover - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Cover ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Cover mobile application ("App").

**Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.** By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **PIN/Password**: We store your vault PIN locally using secure encryption. We never transmit or store your PIN on our servers.
- **Hidden Content**: Photos, videos, files, and apps you choose to hide are encrypted and stored locally on your device.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (for app functionality only)
- **App Usage**: Feature usage statistics (anonymized) to improve the app
- **Crash Reports**: Anonymous crash data to help us fix bugs
- **Intruder Logs**: Photos and location data of unauthorized access attempts (stored locally only)

### 2.3 Firebase Services
We use Firebase for:
- **Remote Configuration**: App settings and feature flags (no personal data)
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics
- **Crashlytics**: Anonymous crash reporting
- **In-App Messaging**: Promotional and informational messages

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **To provide the service**: Encrypting and storing your hidden content
- **To improve the app**: Analyzing anonymous usage data
- **To secure your vault**: Detecting and logging intrusion attempts
- **To communicate**: Sending important security updates (if needed)

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- All your hidden content is stored **locally** on your device
- Encryption uses AES-256-GCM with keys derived from your PIN
- Master keys are stored in Android Keystore (hardware-backed when available)

### 4.2 No Cloud Storage
- We do **NOT** upload your hidden photos, videos, or files to any server
- Your data never leaves your device

### 4.3 Security Measures
- AES-256 encryption for all content
- PBKDF2 key derivation (100,000 iterations)
- Biometric authentication support
- Automatic lock after inactivity
- Intruder detection with photo capture

## 5. Information Sharing

We do **NOT** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Period.

The only exceptions are:
- **Service Providers**: Firebase (Google) for analytics and crash reporting (anonymous data only)
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your hidden content anytime
- **Delete**: Permanently delete any or all hidden content
- **Export**: Move content back to your regular gallery
- **Uninstall**: Remove the app and all associated data

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
